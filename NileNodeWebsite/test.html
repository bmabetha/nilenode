    • Track web traffic, user activity and revenues
    • Moderate user submissions and manage site operations with ease

    • Customize the header and footer of your website and add widgets on the fly
    • Add features and content sections as and when you need
    • Customize site navigation with ease

    Configure your website with ease

    • Allow users to login using their Facebook, Twitter, Linkedin or Google accounts
    • Out of the box support for leading payment gateways like PayPal, Authorize.net and Stripe

    • Easily manage SEO for all sections of the website

    • Publish videos and images from YouTube, Vimeo and flickr

    • Integrate affiliate services like Expedia, Viator and more.

    User activity and revenue reports

    • Track user and staff activity
    • Get deep insights about web traffic through in-built reports plus Google Analytics integration
    • Generate revenue reports for any time interval

    Moderate users and manage staff permissions

    • Moderate user accounts
    • Create staff accounts and assign role based permissions
    • Promote user accounts

    • Curate related content on channel pages and make it easier for users to find content of their interest
