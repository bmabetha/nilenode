About Us

NileNode is a complete and highly capable managed software that focuses on delivering news and media at a scale of thousands of users per month (tested).

NileNode is not a Wordpress site. NileNode is designed from the ground up with a strong focus on performance and security, areas in which most Wordpress sites either fail to deliver or make assumptions and leaves a set of expectations to the user to perform complex work flows such as ensuring security and redundancy of their data.

NileNode is also able to scale in order to support millions of page views while still being fast. NileNode infrastructure is built in such a way that it is able to resize to support your traffic needs and therefore reducing costs involved with overusing computing resources.
